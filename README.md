<h2>🚀 Project Overview</h2>
<p>
This project performs <b>Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)</b> on a large-scale e-commerce delivery dataset
to uncover the key drivers of <b>refund requests, customer satisfaction, and revenue performance</b>.
</p>

<p>
The analysis is designed to simulate a <b>real-world business analyst scenario</b>, where data is used
to identify operational inefficiencies and recommend actionable improvements.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Business Problem</h2>

<p>E-commerce platforms face critical challenges:</p>

<ul>
  <li>High refund rates impacting revenue</li>
  <li>Declining customer satisfaction</li>
  <li>Unclear impact of delivery performance on user experience</li>
</ul>

<p>This project answers:</p>

<ul>
  <li>What drives refund requests?</li>
  <li>Does delivery delay actually impact customer dissatisfaction?</li>
  <li>Which categories and platforms perform best/worst?</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Dataset Overview</h2>

<ul>
  <li>📌 100,000+ transactions</li>
  <li>📌 11 features including delivery time, platform, rating, and refunds</li>
  <li>📌 No missing values (clean dataset)</li>
</ul>

---

<h2>📈 Key Insights</h2>

<ul>
  <li>📦 <b>Swiggy Instamart</b> handles the highest transaction volume</li>
  <li>⚠️ <b>Delivery delay has minimal impact</b> on refund probability</li>
  <li>🛒 <b>Grocery category</b> has the highest refund requests</li>
  <li>💰 <b>Personal Care</b> generates the highest revenue</li>
  <li>⭐ Refund requests are strongly linked to <b>low service ratings</b></li>
  <li>🏆 <b>JioMart</b> has the highest average customer ratings</li>
</ul>

---

<h2>🚨 Critical Business Insight</h2>

<p>
<b>Refunds — not delivery delays — are the primary driver of customer dissatisfaction.</b>
</p>

<p>
This shifts business focus from:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>❌ Speed optimization</li>
  <li>✅ Quality control & service experience</li>
</ul>

---

<h2>💡 Business Recommendations</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Improve quality checks for Grocery and perishable items</li>
  <li>Optimize packaging and fulfillment processes</li>
  <li>Reduce refund-triggering issues to improve ratings</li>
  <li>Benchmark high-performing platforms (JioMart, Swiggy)</li>
</ul>

---

<h2>🧠 Analytical Approach</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Data validation (no null values)</li>
  <li>Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)</li>
  <li>Grouping & aggregation using Pandas</li>
  <li>Visualization using Seaborn & Matplotlib</li>
  <li>Business interpretation of results</li>
</ul>
